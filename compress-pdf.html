Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF compression safe?

Yes! All compression happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded anywhere.

How much can I compress a PDF?

Results vary by content. Image-heavy PDFs can compress 50-90%, while text-only PDFs compress 10-30%.

Does compression affect quality?

Higher compression reduces image quality. Use Quality Based mode to balance size and quality for your needs.

Can I compress multiple PDFs at once?

Yes! Upload multiple files and compress them all at once with the same settings.
