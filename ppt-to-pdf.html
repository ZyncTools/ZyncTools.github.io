PowerPoint vs PDF: Understanding the Formats

PowerPoint (PPT/PPTX) is the industry standard for creating presentations. It offers rich animation, transitions, and interactive elements. However, PowerPoint files require specific software to view and may display differently across devices with different fonts installed.

PDF (Portable Document Format) is the universal standard for document sharing. PDFs display exactly the same on any device, preserve all formatting, and can be viewed in any web browser. Converting to PDF ensures your audience sees your presentation exactly as you designed it.

Converting PowerPoint to PDF gives you the best of both worlds - the professional design of your presentation with the universal accessibility of PDF.
