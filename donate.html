Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZyncPDF really going to stay free forever?

Yes! We're committed to the open-source, privacy-first model. All tools will remain free and unlimited. Your support helps us keep that promise — we never sell user data or lock features behind paywalls.

How does GitHub Sponsors work?

GitHub Sponsors is a safe platform for funding open-source projects. You can sponsor monthly or one-time. Payment is secure, and you can cancel anytime. We receive the funds directly through GitHub.

What if I can't afford to donate?

That's completely okay! ZyncPDF will always be free for everyone. You can support us by sharing the project with others, contributing code, or leaving us a star on GitHub. Every bit helps!

How are funds used?

Funds go directly to server costs, domain renewals, SSL certificates, and compensating development time. We're transparent — you can see exactly where money goes in our quarterly reports (shared with all sponsors).
