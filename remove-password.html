Frequently Asked Questions

Is it legal to remove a password from a PDF?

Yes, it is legal to remove passwords from PDFs that you own or have permission to modify. You should never attempt to unlock PDFs you don't own or have authorization to access.

What if I forgot the password?

This tool requires the current password to unlock the PDF. It cannot recover or crack forgotten passwords. You must know the password to remove it.

Is my password sent to your servers?

No! The entire decryption process happens locally in your browser using pdf-lib. Your password and file data are never uploaded anywhere.

Can this remove owner passwords (restrictions)?

Yes, this tool can remove both user passwords (required to open) and owner passwords (restrictions on editing, printing, etc.).
