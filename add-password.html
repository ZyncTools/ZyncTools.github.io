Technical Details: AES-256 Encryption

AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit key) is the strongest symmetric encryption algorithm publicly available. It's used by:

  • U.S. Government for classified information
  • Banks and financial institutions
  • Military and intelligence agencies
  • Leading cloud storage providers

With current technology, brute-forcing an AES-256 key would take billions of years, making your documents practically impossible to crack without the password.
