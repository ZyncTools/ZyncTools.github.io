Merge PDF — Combine Multiple PDF Files into One

Welcome to ZyncPDF's free PDF merger — the most powerful, private, and completely free solution for combining multiple PDF files into a single document. With drag-and-drop reordering and page previews, you have full control over your merged document.

Unlike other PDF mergers that upload your files to unknown servers, ZyncPDF processes everything directly in your browser. Your documents never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. Merge contracts, reports, presentations, or any PDF files with ease.

Why Choose ZyncPDF for PDF Merging?

  • Drag & Drop Reordering: Easily rearrange files by dragging them into your desired order.
  • Page Previews: See the first page of each PDF as a thumbnail for easy identification.
  • 100% Private: All processing happens locally. No uploads, no servers.
  • Completely Free: No limits, no paywalls, no registration required.
  • Unlimited Files: Merge as many PDFs as you need — no restrictions.
  • Fast & Efficient: Optimized merging technology for instant results.

How to Merge PDF Files

  1. Upload Your PDFs: Drag and drop your PDF files or click to select them.
  2. Reorder Files: Drag and drop files in the list to arrange them in your preferred order.
  3. Merge: Click "Merge PDF Files" to combine all documents into one.
  4. Download: Get your merged PDF instantly — no waiting, no uploads.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reorder files before merging?

Yes! Simply drag and drop any file in the list to change its position. The merged PDF will follow this order.

Is there a limit to how many PDFs I can merge?

No limits! Merge as many PDFs as you need. Performance depends on your device's memory and file sizes.

Does merging affect PDF quality?

No, merging is a lossless operation. The quality, formatting, and content of each page remain exactly as in the original files.

Is my data safe when merging PDFs?

Absolutely! All merging happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
