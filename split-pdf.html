Split PDF - Free PDF Splitter & Page Extractor

Welcome to ZyncPDF's free PDF splitter - the most flexible, private, and completely free solution for splitting PDF files. Choose from three powerful modes: split every page, extract custom ranges, or visually select pages with live previews.

Unlike other PDF splitters that upload your files to unknown servers, ZyncPDF processes everything directly in your browser. Your documents never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Why Choose ZyncPDF for PDF Splitting?

  • 📄 Three Split Modes: Split by pages, custom ranges, or visual page selection with previews.
  • 🎯 Visual Page Selection: See thumbnails of each page and click to select which pages to extract.
  • 📚 Merge Option: Combine selected ranges or pages into a single PDF file.
  • 🔒 100% Private: All processing happens locally. No uploads, no servers.
  • 💰 Completely Free: No limits, no paywalls, no registration required.
  • 📦 ZIP Download: All split files are bundled into a convenient ZIP archive.

How to Split PDF Files

  1. Upload Your PDF: Drag and drop your PDF or click to select it.
  2. Choose Split Mode: Select Split by Pages, Split by Range, or Select Pages.
  3. Configure Options: Enter ranges or click on page previews to select pages.
  4. Split & Download: Click "Split PDF" and download your extracted pages as a ZIP file.

Split Modes Explained

📄 Split by Pages: Creates one PDF per page. Perfect for extracting individual pages from a large document.

✂️ Split by Range: Extract specific page ranges like "1-5, 8, 10-15". Each range becomes a separate PDF.

☑️ Select Pages: Visual page previews - click on thumbnails to select exactly which pages to extract.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract specific pages from a PDF?

Yes! Use the "Select Pages" mode to see thumbnails of each page and click to choose which ones to extract.

Can I merge extracted pages into one PDF?

Yes! When using Range or Select Pages mode, check the "Merge" option to combine all extracted pages into a single PDF file.

Is my PDF uploaded to your servers?

Never. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files stay on your device.

What format are the split files delivered in?

All split files are packaged into a single ZIP file for easy one-click download.
